## ğŸ” Project Insights

### Sales Insights

* Clear **year-over-year growth comparison** across sales, profit, and quantity enabled quick performance evaluation.
* Certain subcategories showed **high sales but low profit**, indicating discount or cost issues.
* Weekly trend analysis helped detect **seasonal fluctuations** and campaign impact.

* Sales per customer metric helped evaluate **customer lifetime value trends**.
* Monthly comparison helped track **customer acquisition and engagement changes**.
